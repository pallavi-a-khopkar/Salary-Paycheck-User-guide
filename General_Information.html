General Information

This topic covers the information to be entered in the ‘General Information’ section of the tool.



Field Description
Check Date Date on which check is issued to employee.
State State location of the business for which the employee is working.
Gross Pay Gross Pay: Total annual pay of the employee as agreed upon contract, prior to any deductions.
Gross Pay Method The method of payment which can be annual or pay-per-period. Annual payments are done once a year. Pay-per-period option exists when employee is paid at any frequency other than annual.
Pay frequency This is the frequency at which employer would pay the employee. Choose a suitable option from the drop down list.
Federal Tax exemption Federal tax exemption is applicable to employees that have no tax liability for the previous year and must expect to have no tax liability for the current year. A Form W-4 claiming exemption from withholding is valid for only the calendar year in which it's furnished to the employer. Select Yes, if exemption is applicable.
FICA exemption FICA exemption is applicable to international students, scholars, professors, teachers, trainees, researchers, physicians, au pairs, summer camp workers, and other aliens temporarily present in the United States in F-1,J-1,M-1, or Q-1/Q-2 non immigrant status, as long as such services are allowed by USCIS.Note that this section of the tool calculates Social Security tax at the rate of 6.2%. Medicare tax is calculated separately depending upon eligibility. Select Yes, if exemption is applicable.
Medicare exemption Medicare withholding is a subset of FICA, FICA includes 1.45% contribution for Medicare and 6.2% for Social security. An employee can be eligible to pay social security tax under FICA but exempt from medicare. Select Yes, if exemption is applicable.
