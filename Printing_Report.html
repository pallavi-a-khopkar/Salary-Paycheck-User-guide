Printing Report

This topic provides information on how to print the generated report

Select one of the options from the drop down list for printing the report.

  • Yes-All Reports
  • Yes-Check on top
  • Yes-Check in Middle
  • Yes-Check in bottom
  • Yes-Earning records
  • No

Once the selection is made the employer will be prompted to enter employee information like employee id, employee name, address etc.

Email option is also available where if the information is entered an email will be sent to the employer.
