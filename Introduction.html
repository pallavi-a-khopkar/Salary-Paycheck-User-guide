Introduction

Salary Paycheck Calculator aids employers calculate their employees take-home salary.

Gross salary is subject to exemptions and withholdings as per the Federal and State tax rules. Net pay is different than the gross pay agreed upon by the employer and the employee. Salary Paycheck Calculator eases the task of calculating the net take home salary of the employees based on their gross pay subject to deductions and withholdings.The following user guide gives a detailed explaination for using the Salary Paycheck Calculator.
